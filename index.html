MONTANA OF 300

ALL ABOUT MONTANA OF 300

Walter Anthony Bradford born March 3,1987, better known professionally as Monana Of 300 or simply Montana, is an American rapper and songwriter from Peoria, Illinois, his debut studio album, fire in the church, was released on may 20, 2016.

ALBUMS

MONTANA'S RAP GROUP

Montana is a member of the rap group 300 which was created in 2008 and is also in another group called F.G.E. which stands for Fly Guy Entertainment.
